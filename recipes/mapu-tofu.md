## Instructions

1. Fry garlic and Sichuan pepper in oil.
2. Add spring onion and mapo tofu sauce; stir briefly.
3. Add tofu, sauces, wine, and sugar; simmer a few minutes.
4. Finish with sesame oil; top with coriander.
