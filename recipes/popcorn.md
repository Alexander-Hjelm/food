# How to: popcorn

> ?

## Tags

- snack, vegetarian, vegan, guide

## Ingredients

| Ingredient             | Amount      | Prep         | Optional |
| ---------------------- | ----------- | ------------ | -------- |
| Popcorn kernels        | 0.5dl       |              |          |
| Salt                   | 1/2 tsp     |              |          |
| Cooking oil            | 1 tsp       |              | Optional |

## Instructions

1. Oil up a pot and bring up to medium heat (2/3-2.5/3).
2. Add 2-3 test kernels to the pot.
3. As soon as the test kernels pop, take the pot off the heat and add enough kernels to cover the bottom of the pot. Reference: 0.5dl per person.
4. Bring the pot up to heat again. Wait until the popping slows down to 1 pop per second and remove from heat. Let the rest of the popping finish on its own.
5. Remember to shake the pot vigourously through the whole process, especially if popping without oil.
