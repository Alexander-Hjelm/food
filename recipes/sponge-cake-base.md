# Sponge Cake Base

> I don't remember what this is for, but it's not my go-to cake base recipe.

## Tags

- dessert, baking, cake, swedish

## Ingredients

| Ingredient       | Amount     | Prep         | Optional |
| ---------------- | ---------- | ------------ | -------- |
| Eggs             | 4          | separated    |          |
| Powdered sugar   | 20 g       |              |          |
| Milk             | 40 g       |              |          |
| Vegetable oil    | 40 g       |              |          |
| Wheat flour      | 40 g       |              |          |

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
2. Separate the egg whites and yolks into two bowls.
3. Whisk the egg whites with powdered sugar, adding the sugar gradually in three portions, until stiff peaks form.
4. In a separate bowl, mix the egg yolks with milk, vegetable oil, and flour until smooth.
5. Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the egg yolk mixture, taking care not to deflate the batter.
6. Pour the batter into a prepared baking pan.
7. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes or until lightly golden and springy to the touch.
8. Let cool completely before using for your cake.
