## Instructions

1. The amount is the reccomended serving for 1 unit of **Alexander** who is:
   - Reasonably hungry
   - Has done a small amount of training
   - Has normal stress levels
2. Adjust the final portion size as needed depending on how hungry your **Alexander** is. Remember to not feed it after midnight!
