# Potato Chips

> ?

## Tags

- snack, side dish, vegetarian, fried

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount | Prep | Optional |
| ---------- | ------ | ---- | -------- |
| Potatoes   | 500g   | thinly sliced with mandolin |          |
| Salt       | to taste | for soaking and seasoning |          |
| Oil        | enough for deep frying | neutral oil |          |

## Instructions

1. Slice the potatoes very thinly using a mandolin on the thinnest setting.
2. Rinse the potato slices in lukewarm water several times until the water runs clear to remove excess starch.
3. Soak the slices in a salty water solution for at least 10 minutes.
4. Drain and pat the slices dry thoroughly with paper towels.
5. Heat oil in a deep fryer or pan and fry the potato slices in batches until golden and crispy.
6. Drain the fried chips in a colander to remove excess oil.
7. Season with salt or your preferred seasoning while still warm and serve immediately.
