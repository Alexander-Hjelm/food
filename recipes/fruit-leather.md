# Fruit Leather

> ?

## Tags

- snack, dessert, healthy, vegan, fruit

## Ingredients

| Ingredient    | Amount      | Prep     | Optional |
| ------------- | ----------- | -------- | -------- |
| Banana        | 1 large     | mashed   |          |
| Mixed fruit or berries | 500g | pureed  |          |

## Instructions

1. Blend the banana and fruit/berries into a smooth puree.  
2. Spread the puree thinly on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.  
3. Dry in an oven at 50°C for 9–12 hours until pliable.  
4. Cut into strips and enjoy.
