# How to: Syrup for Drinks

> ?

## Tags

- guide, drinks

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount | Prep | Optional |
| ---------- | ------ | ---- | -------- |
| Sugar | 100 g | | |
| Water | 100 ml | | |

## Instructions

1. Combine sugar and water in a saucepan.
2. Heat while stirring until the sugar is fully dissolved and the mixture comes to a boil.
3. Remove from heat and let cool completely.
4. Store in a sealed container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
