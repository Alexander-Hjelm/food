| ----------------- | ------------- | ----------------------- | -------- |
| Chicken pieces      | 500 g         | cut into bite-sized pieces |          |
| Wheat flour         | 100 g         | for coating              |          |

| Salt                | to taste      | for sauce               |          |

## Instructions

1. Prepare the batter by mixing flour, egg, and water until slightly liquid.
2. Dip the chicken pieces into the batter.
3. Deep-fry the chicken in hot oil until golden, then remove and let cool slightly.
4. Deep-fry the chicken a second time to ensure extra crispiness.
5. Prepare the sauce by combining vinegar, ketchup, sugar, and salt in a saucepan. Bring to a boil.
6. Toss the fried chicken in the hot sauce until fully coated.
7. Serve immediately for maximum crunch and flavor.
