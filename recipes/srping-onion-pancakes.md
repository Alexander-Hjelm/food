# Spring Onion Pancakes

> ?

## Tags

- chinese, asian, snack, vegetarian, appetizer

## Ingredients

| Ingredient       | Amount       | Prep                  | Optional |
| ---------------- | ----------- | -------------------- | -------- |
| All-purpose flour | 300g        |                      |          |
| Boiling water     | 200ml       |                      |          |
| Spring onions     | 3-4 stalks  | finely chopped       |          |
| White oil         | 2 tbsp      | for frying           |          |
| Sesame oil        | 1 tbsp      | for frying           |          |
| Soy sauce         | 2 tbsp      | for serving          | Optional |
| Chili sauce       | 2 tbsp      | for serving          | Optional |

## Instructions

1. Mix the flour with boiling water in a 3:2 ratio to form a dough. Knead until smooth and let rest for 30 minutes.
2. Roll the dough into thin flatbreads and sprinkle finely chopped spring onions evenly over each.
3. Heat a mix of white oil and sesame oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry each pancake until golden and crispy on both sides.
4. Serve hot with soy sauce or chili sauce for dipping.
