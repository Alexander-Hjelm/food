# Pomelo Honey Tea

> ?

## Tags

- asian, tea, beverage, fruit, sweet

## Ingredients

| Ingredient       | Amount       | Prep                        | Optional |
| ---------------- | ----------- | --------------------------- | -------- |
| Pomelo           | 1           | peeled, segmented, rind removed |          |
| Honey             | 2 tbsp      |                             |          |
| Water             | 500 ml      |                             |          |
| Salt              | a pinch     | for washing pomelo          |          |

## Instructions

1. Rinse the pomelo with saltwater to clean it.
2. Peel the pomelo, removing the outer rind.
3. Separate the fruit segments and set aside one-third of the pulp. Place the pulp and the peeled rind in a saucepan.
4. Add honey to the saucepan and heat on high until it begins to simmer.
5. Reduce heat to medium and stir occasionally, letting it cook down until almost all water has evaporated.
6. Pour the concentrated mixture into a jar and let cool before storing.
7. Serve hot or mix with hot water for a fragrant pomelo honey tea.
