# Pirogi

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount | Prep | Optional |
|-----------|--------|------|----------|
| Fresh yeast | 50 g | crumbled | |
| Milk | 500 ml | warmed | |
| Flour | 12 dl | | |
| Salt | 1 tsp | | |
| Sugar | 1 tsp | | |
| Butter | 50 g | melted | |
| Egg | 1 | for brushing | |
| Filling of choice | – | e.g. meat, vegetables, cheese | Optional |

## Instructions

1. Warm the milk and butter together until lukewarm. Do **not** add the flour yet.
2. Crumble the yeast into a bowl and pour in the warm milk–butter mixture. Stir until dissolved.
3. Add salt, sugar, and the flour gradually. Knead until a smooth dough forms.
4. Let the dough rise for **30 minutes**.
5. Roll out the dough and cut into round pieces of about **150 g each**.
6. Add your desired filling along with some cheese if you like.
7. Brush the edges with beaten egg, fold over, and seal the pirog firmly.
8. Brush the entire pirog with egg and let rise **10 minutes** more.
9. Bake at **200°C** for **15 minutes** or until golden brown.
