| Ginger              | 1 tsp  | minced   | Optional |

## Instructions

1. Dice tofu into ~2 cm cubes.
2. Lay tofu on paper towels for 30 min to drain; press with a book if needed.
3. Cut dried tofu into desired size.
4. Combine tofu and marinade in a freezer bag; marinate for at least 1 hour.
