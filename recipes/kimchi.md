# Kimchi

> ?

## Tags

- korean, fermented, side dish, vegetarian, condiment

## Ingredients

| Ingredient          | Amount      | Prep                    | Optional |
| ------------------- | ----------- | ---------------------- | -------- |
| Chinese cabbage     | 1 medium    | chopped                 |          |
| Onion               | 1–2        | sliced                  | Optional |
| Salt                | 3–4 tbsp    | for salting             |          |
| Kimchi sauce        | 3–4 tbsp    | store-bought or homemade |          |
| Cumin seeds         | 1 tsp       |                        | Optional |
| Chili pepper        | 1–2 tsp     | crushed or powdered     | Optional |

## Instructions

1. Chop the Chinese cabbage and onion into bite-sized pieces.
2. Evenly coat the vegetables with a generous amount of salt.
3. Let the salted vegetables sit for 2 hours to draw out moisture.
4. Rinse the salt off thoroughly and gently squeeze out excess water.
5. Mix in kimchi sauce, cumin, and chili pepper until well combined.
6. Store in a clean jar and let ferment at room temperature for 1–2 days, or refrigerate immediately for a milder flavor.
7. Serve as a spicy, tangy side dish or condiment.
