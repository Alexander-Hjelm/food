## Instructions

1. Soak chickpeas overnight. Blend chickpeas, onion, parsley, garlic, flour, and spices in a food processor.
2. Chill mixture in fridge for a few hours. Form into balls.
3. Fry in hot oil until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels. If falafel falls apart, roll in flour or egg before frying.
